# Explicit Non-Goals

Stating what this library will *not* do is as important as stating what it will. Non-goals
protect the method from scope creep and set honest expectations. Each is a deliberate
boundary, not an oversight.

## Not a ranking or leaderboard

The library does not produce "best AI product" lists or single-number ratings. Products are
analysed on six dimensions with qualitative, bounded judgements. Reducing that to a rank
would manufacture false precision (see
[`frameworks/scoring-false-precision.md`](../../frameworks/scoring-false-precision.md)).

## Not investment or financial advice

Teardowns describe product behaviour and evidence. They do not value companies, forecast
revenue, or recommend buying, selling or funding anything. Business-model analysis explains
*how a product appears to create and capture value*, not whether it is a good investment.

## Not marketing, PR, or thought-leadership

The library has no promotional relationship with any product it covers. It does not court
vendors, publish sponsored content, or optimise for reach. Conflicts of interest are
declared under governance.

## Not a real-time news tracker

We do not attempt to report every release the day it ships. Teardowns are dated snapshots,
updated deliberately under a versioning policy. Freshness is a property we manage, not a
race we run.

## Not a reproduction of proprietary or private material

The library does not host leaked, paywalled, or confidential material, and does not
reproduce copyrighted content beyond brief, attributed quotation. See the privacy and
sensitive-information policy under governance.

## Not a settled or authoritative verdict

Every teardown is a best current judgement from available evidence, explicitly open to
dispute. The library values being *correctable* over being *authoritative*. A teardown that
cannot be challenged has failed its own standard.

## Not opinion journalism

Personal taste, vibe, and unsupported prediction are out of scope. Where judgement is
required, it is labelled as inference or hypothesis and tied to evidence and a confidence
level. Opinion that cannot be grounded this way does not belong in the library.

## Not (in Phase 1) a website or search product

Phase 1 builds the research foundation and validation tooling only. A searchable frontend
is explicitly deferred to a later phase; see the [roadmap](../roadmap/roadmap.md).
