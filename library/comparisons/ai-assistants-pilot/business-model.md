# Cross-Product Business-Model Comparison

> **No global ranking; no revenue speculation.** Compares documented packaging and value-capture
> choices only. Prices are region- and time-sensitive. Scope: consumer web surface,
> documentation-first, 2026-07-13.

## Comparison 1 — Tier structure

- **ChatGPT:** Free, Go, Plus, Pro (+ Business/Enterprise) (ev: cg-plans-overview) — the most
  granular consumer ladder, including a low-cost **Go** tier.
- **Claude:** Free, Pro, Max (+ Team/Enterprise) (ev: cl-plans-pricing).
- **Perplexity:** Standard (Free), Pro, Max, Education Pro (+ Enterprise Pro/Max, Sonar API)
  (ev: px-plans-overview) — includes an explicit **student** tier.
- **Confidence:** High (documented). **Limitation:** tiers only; not per-plan behaviour.

## Comparison 2 — Price points (as documented; region-sensitive)

- **ChatGPT:** Plus **$20/mo (US help article)** vs **₹1,999/mo (observed INR page)** — pricing
  is **region-localised** (ev: cg-plus-identity-price, cg-pricing-region-inr).
- **Claude:** Pro **$17/mo annual ($20 monthly)**; Max **from $100**; Team from **$20/seat**
  (USD) (ev: cl-pro-price, cl-max-team-plans).
- **Perplexity:** only **Education Pro $10/mo** stated verbatim on read pages; standard consumer
  Pro/Max prices are an **evidence gap** (ev: px-education-pro-price).
- **Scope difference:** direct price comparison is **partially invalid** — ChatGPT read in INR,
  Claude in USD, Perplexity consumer prices missing. We do **not** merge these.
- **Confidence:** High for the figures shown; the comparison itself is **limited by region and
  gaps**.

## Comparison 3 — Value capture mechanics

- **ChatGPT:** subscription + usage-based Pro (5x/20x) + **advertising on a paid tier (Go)**
  (ev: cg-pro-usage-tiers, cg-ads-go-plan, cg-ads-controls).
- **Claude:** subscription escalating to **usage-scaled seats** (Max/Team/Enterprise seat +
  usage at API rates) (ev: cl-max-team-plans, cl-enterprise-usage-pricing); **no advertising
  evidenced**.
- **Perplexity:** subscription + **separately billed API**; resells third-party models, implying
  partial pass-through costs (ev: px-max-tier, px-model-choice).
- **Headline contrast:** only **ChatGPT** shows advertising (on a *paid* tier); Claude and
  Perplexity monetise via subscription/usage as evidenced. Confidence: High that ads exist on
  ChatGPT Go; **absence elsewhere is not proven** (gap).

## Comparison 4 — Switching costs

- **Claude:** documents **portable memory** (import/export) that *lowers* switching costs
  (ev: cl-memory-import-export) — unusual and notable.
- **ChatGPT:** deletion/memory spans many surfaces (ev: cg-memory-delete); portability not
  documented.
- **Perplexity:** memory (Brain) gated/preview; portability not documented.
- **Confidence:** Moderate for Claude's stance; others are gaps, not negatives.

## What cannot be concluded (business model)

- No revenue, margin, valuation, or "which is the better business" claims.
- No merged cross-region price ranking (region/gap issues make it partly invalid).
- Whether advertising exists on Claude/Perplexity — not evidenced either way.
